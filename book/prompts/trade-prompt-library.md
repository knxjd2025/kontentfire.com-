---

## How to Use This Library

1. **Start with your Brand Voice Prompt.** Every prompt below includes a placeholder: `[PASTE YOUR BRAND VOICE PROMPT HERE]`. Replace it with the brand voice prompt you built in Chapter 3. This ensures every piece of content sounds like *you*, not a robot.

2. **Fill in the placeholders.** Replace `[CITY]`, `[BUSINESS NAME]`, and any other bracketed items with your actual information.

3. **Copy, paste, generate.** Drop the completed prompt into your AI tool and review the output. Edit for accuracy, add your personal touch, and publish.

## Pro Tips for Getting Better Results

1. **Layer your prompts.** Run the base prompt first, then follow up with "Now make it more conversational" or "Add a local reference to [LANDMARK] in [CITY]."

2. **Generate variations.** After getting one version, ask: "Give me 3 alternative hooks for this same post." Pick the strongest one.

3. **Adapt across trades.** See a prompt structure you like in the Plumbing section but you are a roofer? Copy the structure and change the trade-specific details.

4. **Seasonal rotation.** Set a calendar reminder to pull the seasonal prompts 2-3 weeks before each season starts so your content is ready when your customers are thinking about it.

5. **Batch your content.** Run 5-7 prompts in one sitting to generate a week or two of content at once. Review, edit, and schedule. This is your content engine.
